## Overview

Affinity Photo Advanced is a professional photo editing application from Serif that provides comprehensive tools for RAW development, photo retouching, HDR merging, panorama stitching, and digital painting — all available as a one-time purchase without the recurring subscription that competing professional tools require. It has established itself as the most capable alternative to subscription-based editors, earning professional adoption across photography, design, and publishing. The application runs natively on Apple Silicon with performance that rivals or exceeds subscription competitors on equivalent hardware.

Persona-based workflow organizes editing tools into focused modes — Photo, Liquify, Develop, Tone Mapping, Export — each presenting the specific tools relevant to that stage of the editing process, keeping the interface clean and contextually appropriate. Switching between personas preserves the full editing state, enabling fluid transitions between RAW development, retouching, and export without losing work or context. This modal approach reduces interface clutter by showing only what is needed for the current task.

RAW development persona provides full-featured RAW processing with exposure, white balance, curves, HSL, split toning, noise reduction, and lens corrections, producing developed images that match the quality of dedicated RAW processing applications. The RAW engine processes files from all major camera manufacturers and supports the latest camera models through regular updates. Batch processing of RAW files enables consistent development settings across entire shoots.

Live filter layers apply non-destructive effects including blur, sharpen, lighting, and distortion as editable layers that can be modified, masked, or removed at any point without affecting the underlying image data. Each live filter retains its own mask and blend mode, enabling precise control over how effects interact with the underlying image. The non-destructive nature of live filters means that creative decisions can be revised at any stage of the editing process without starting over.

For photographers and image editors who want professional-grade editing capability without an ongoing subscription commitment, Affinity Photo Advanced is the one-time-purchase alternative that delivers professional results without the professional pricing model. Its combination of editing depth, performance, and fair pricing has earned it a dedicated following among professionals who value ownership of their creative tools.

## Performance

Affinity Photo balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your system responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series chips, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
